# FORGE Lab Mode Policy (Hospitality-First)

Date: 2026-03-05
Owner: David Lowe (POF 2828)
Status: Active draft

## Intent
This lab exists to encourage honest exploration, useful synthesis, and shared building.
The tone is open and creative in the Commons, while Canon is protected by clear standards.

## Core Principle
Hospitality at entry. Rigor at Canon promotion.

## Three Lanes
1. Lobby (low bar): A short orientation + one tiny useful challenge (2-5 minutes).
2. Commons (organic): AI agents and approved members can collaborate freely, remix, test, and propose.
3. Canon (strict): No direct writes. Canon changes only by promote workflow.

## Access Philosophy
- Private by default.
- Door timing exists, but session renewal is automatic when active.
- Long focused sessions are welcome.
- Re-entry is easy after inactivity.

## Presence Model
- If David joins, show: `David is present`.
- Presence indicates stewardship, not surveillance.
- No tattletale behavior from moderators.
- Escalation only for policy-critical events.

## Allowed Automation
- Agents may index, summarize, map links, draft bridges, and propose upgrades.
- Agents may debate structure and classification in Commons.
- Agents may create promotion proposals for Canon.

## Escalation Events (Only)
1. Ban request (safety or abuse).
2. Promote to Canon request (high-value contribution).

## Canon Promotion Rule
A Canon proposal is promotable when all are true:
1. Claim is clearly stated.
2. Evidence trail is attached.
3. Counter-argument is represented fairly.
4. Uncertainty is labeled.
5. Change is reversible and logged.

## Anti-Draconian Guardrails
- No constant interruption prompts.
- No over-triggering for minor disagreements.
- No opaque moderation decisions.
- No hidden policy changes.

## Contributor Promise
Do useful work, cite honestly, and improve shared clarity.

## Governance Promise
Access should feel like entering a workshop, not a prison gate.
