# Front Questions (User Visible)

1. What belief or question are we testing today?
2. What is your current lean and confidence (0-100)?
3. What would count as strong evidence against your current lean?
4. What alternative views must be included?
5. Which sources are in-bounds and out-of-bounds?
6. After reviewing this source, what changed (if anything)?
7. What is your current best conclusion and why?
8. What is the strongest objection still standing?
9. What evidence would change your mind?
10. Final confidence (0-100) and unresolved items?
